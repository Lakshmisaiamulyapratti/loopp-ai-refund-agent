# Loopp Commerce Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Rules
1. Customers may request a refund within 30 days of delivery.
2. The item must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging.
3. Refunds are allowed only for orders with status `delivered`.
4. Refunds are not allowed for final-sale items, gift cards, downloadable digital products, or personalized products.
5. If an item arrived damaged or incorrect, a refund may be approved within 45 days if evidence is provided.
6. Customers with more than 3 refunds in the last 12 months require manual review.
7. Orders with fraud flags must be denied and escalated to support review.
8. Refunds above $500 require manual review even if other conditions are satisfied.
9. If return tracking is required, the refund is approved conditionally after return shipment is confirmed.

## Agent Requirements
- The agent must not approve refunds that violate policy.
- The agent must explain decisions clearly and politely.
- The agent must use CRM/order data before making a decision.
- When policy is ambiguous, the agent should request clarification or escalate.
